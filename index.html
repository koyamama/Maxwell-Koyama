✄ Writer / Editor / Project Hub ✄

    Dear visitor, thank you for navigating to my website. I
am a writer and editor based in Toronto. My work has ap-
peared in Poem Shorts in 2021, Acta Victoriana in 2020 &
2018, Half a Grapefruit Magazine in 2020 and 2019, and in
Ink and Voices in 2018.

    I hold a master's degree in Creative Writing and English
from the University of Toronto, awarded in 2021. Since 2020,
I have been an editor at Half a Grapefruit Magazine. In 2021,
I was the co-editor-in-chief and designer for Issue Nineteen
of Echolocation Magazine. I was also an assistant editor of
the Undergraduate Journal of American Studies at the Unive-
rsity of Toronto for its 2017-2018 issue.

    My other projects include this compilation of recordings
made by ethnomusicologist Alan Lomax in Virginia, and fur-
ther work with archival materials forthcoming.

    Have a nice day,

    ~Maxwell
